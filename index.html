Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I restore a purchase? Reinstall the app and sign in with the same Apple ID or Google account. Your purchase should restore automatically.
  • My progress was lost. Progress is stored on your device. If you uninstalled or switched devices, it cannot be recovered.
  • How do I turn off notifications? Use your device Settings → Split Master → Notifications.
