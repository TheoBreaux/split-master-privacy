Privacy Policy – Split Master

Last updated: February 2026

Split Master ("we", "our", or "the app") is a casual game. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Purchase information. When you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed by Google Play (on Android) or the App Store (on iOS). We use RevenueCat to manage purchases and entitlements. RevenueCat receives purchase-related data from the store (e.g., transaction IDs) to verify your access. See RevenueCat's privacy policy for details.

Local data. The app stores data on your device only (e.g., high score, game progress, sound preferences). This data is not sent to our servers.

Push notifications. We use local notifications to remind you when a free round is available. No account or personal data is required for this.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email, or other personal information. We do not use analytics or advertising trackers.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

  • RevenueCat – for in-app purchase management. Their privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Google Play / App Store – for processing payments. Their policies apply to purchase data.

Data Security

Purchase data is handled by the app stores and RevenueCat. Local data stays on your device.

Children

Split Master is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at your preferred support channel (e.g., via the app listing or developer contact).
